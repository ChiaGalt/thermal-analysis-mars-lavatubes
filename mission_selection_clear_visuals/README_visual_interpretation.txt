MISSION CANDIDATE SELECTION VISUALS

The final mission score combines:
- absolute THEMIS thermal inertia
- positive local thermal anomaly Delta_TI
- local TI z-score
- morphological type usefulness
- catalog priority
- available geometry information such as diameter/depth
